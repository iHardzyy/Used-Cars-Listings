Official Purchase Terms & Conditions

To ensure clarity and transparency for all our valued customers, Pre Owned Motors has established the following policies. Please review these terms carefully before completing a transaction.

1. All Sales Are Final

Every vehicle purchase conducted at Pre Owned Motors is considered final at the point of sale. Once the transaction is complete and ownership is transferred, the sale cannot be reversed. We encourage all customers to be fully certain of their decision before finalizing any purchase.

2. No Refund or Exchange Policy

In line with our "All Sales Are Final" policy, we do not offer refunds, returns, or exchanges on any vehicle purchased from our dealership. This policy is firm and applies to all transactions without exception.

3. Vehicle Information and "As-Is" Condition

All information provided for our vehicles is presented accurately to the best of our knowledge. However, all vehicles are sold in "AS-IS" condition. This means:

  • The vehicle is sold in its current state at the time of purchase.
  • The dealership makes no warranties, expressed or implied, regarding its condition, reliability, or performance post-sale.
  • We highly recommend that prospective buyers conduct their own due diligence, including test drives and independent inspections, before purchase.

4. Customer Acknowledgment

By completing a purchase with Pre Owned Motors, you, the customer, acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to the terms outlined in this policy.
